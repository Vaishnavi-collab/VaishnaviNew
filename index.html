Welcome to Chandigarh University

Chandigarh University(CU) is a private university accredited with A+ Grade by NAAC. There are 16 departments in total offering many specializations in undergraduate, postgraduate and PhD courses.

The university has emerged as one of the top universities in terms of placement and is also recognized and acknowledged by various organisations for its high placement record.

With more than 691 Multi-Nationals visiting CU Campus in 2018-2019, the number of offers touched 6617 and the highest package has registered a growth of 30%, scaling to a new high of INR 35 LPA.
